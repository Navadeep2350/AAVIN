DESCRIPTION
Aavin Mysore pa is prepared by dissolving sugar in water, blending gram flour & skimmed milk powder with ghee and allowing thickening. The contents are then spread in tray for cooling which is then cut to 250 g and 500 g. Shelf life: 12 days.
