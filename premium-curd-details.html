DESCRIPTION
Aavin premium curd is prepared by carefully standardizing milk solids, adding active culture at 45°C and incubating at 42-45°C which is reformed to curd after four hours of setting time. After this, a thick and delicious curd is obtained that comes in variants of 200g, 400g and 1Kg. Shelf life: 12 days for cup curd 200g & 8 days for 400g and 1Kg. Store below 40°C.
